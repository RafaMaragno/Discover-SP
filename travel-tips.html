Travel Tips for São Paulo

Planning a trip to São Paulo? Here are the most common questions international visitors have when exploring São Paulo. Discover our practical advice and recommendations from trusted partners.

Is São Paulo safe for tourists?

São Paulo is a huge metropolis (over 12 million people in the metro area), which means there are safe, modern districts and some areas best avoided at night. Follow some big-city precautions: stay in well-reviewed areas like Paulista, Jardins and Itaim Bibi, avoid isolated places after dark, keep phone and wallet in front pockets or inside a zipped bag, carry only needed cash (cards are widely accepted) and choose licensed transport.

How to get from GRU Airport to downtown?

São Paulo–Guarulhos (GRU) is the main international airport, located about 25 km from the city center. Here are the main ways to reach downtown.

  • Private Transfer: Reliable and safe, especially for late-night arrivals and large luggage.
  • Taxi / Ride Apps: Use official taxi stands at airports or ride-hailing apps (Uber, 99)
  • Airport Bus: Cheaper, goes to Paulista and Barra Funda.
  • Train + Metro: Very cheap, but not ideal with luggage.

How to buy a SIM card in São Paulo?

Prepaid SIMs are sold by carriers like Vivo, TIM, and Claro. Bring your passport for registration. For convenience, eSIM services such as Airalo or Yesim can be activated online before arrival.

Quick Tips

Is it safe to walk at night?

Walking at night is fine in well-lit areas like Paulista, Jardins, Itaim Bibi or Vila Madalena. Avoid walking alone in little-used side streets and always prefer a ride if you're unsure.

Can I drink tap water?

Tap water in São Paulo is generally treated and safe, but there's a risk of it becoming contaminated by impurities found in poorly maintained pipes and water tanks. Therefore, always choose water that has been filtered or purified.

What number to call in an emergency?

Police: 190 / Ambulance: 192 / Fire: 193.
Keep your accommodation address written down in Portuguese to show if needed.
