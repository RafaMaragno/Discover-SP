Culture & Museums

Discover the artistic soul of São Paulo — from iconic museums to underground galleries.

Top Cultural Spots in São Paulo

São Paulo is home to some of the most influential museums in Latin America. Start at the MASP with its suspended architecture and impressive collection, then explore the Pinacoteca and its modern Brazilian art.

Don’t miss hidden gems like the Afro Brasil Museum and Japan House along Avenida Paulista. If you're into street culture, head to Beco do Batman for colorful urban art and spontaneous music shows.
