How to get from GRU to downtown São Paulo?

São Paulo–Guarulhos (GRU) is the main international airport, located about 25 km from the city center. Here are the main ways to reach downtown.

Private Transfer

Door-to-door, safest, recommended for groups or first visits. Fixed price, meet & greet at arrivals.
(Place for image)

Book transfer

Taxi & Ride Apps

Official taxis available at arrivals, or use Uber/99 apps. Cost around R$120–180 depending on traffic.
(Place for image)

Airport Bus Service

Executive buses run from GRU to key downtown points (Paulista, Barra Funda). Cheaper, but less flexible.
(Place for image)

Train & Metro

GRU has a train line linking to São Paulo’s metro. Very cheap, but not ideal with luggage or late-night arrivals.
